UX Design: Time-Controlled Coffee Lid

Solving the problem of overly hot coffee with user-friendly design.

Problem

Many coffee drinkers face the issue of their coffee being too hot to drink after several hours due to excellent heat retention. This causes inconvenience and delays enjoyment.

Solution

We designed a time-controlled coffee lid with a small opening for steam release. The size of the opening is optimized to prevent spills while efficiently reducing the coffee's temperature.

UX Design Process

  1. Research: Studied user needs and heat retention challenges.
  2. Ideation: Designed a lid that allows time-based steam release.
  3. Prototyping: Created a functional prototype with adjustable vents.
  4. Testing: Tested with coffee enthusiasts to gather feedback and refine.
